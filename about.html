About Mov3m3nt

Our story, our music, our journey

Kalul3t3

Mov3m3nt, also known as Kalul3t3, is a Zambian music collective that has been making waves in the African music scene since 2018. Formed in Lusaka, the group brings together diverse musical influences to create a unique sound that blends traditional Zambian rhythms with contemporary hip-hop, afrobeats, and dancehall.

Our Journey

Starting from small performances in local clubs, Mov3m3nt quickly gained recognition for their energetic live shows and innovative sound. Their breakthrough came with the release of [Hit Song Name] in 2020, which became a nationwide hit and established them as one of Zambia's most exciting musical acts.

Since then, they've performed at major festivals across Africa, collaborated with international artists, and amassed a dedicated fanbase known as "The Movement."

Musical Style

Mov3m3nt's music is characterized by its fusion of traditional Zambian kalindula rhythms with modern production techniques. Their lyrics often address themes of unity, social issues, and celebration of African culture, delivered in a mix of English and local languages.
